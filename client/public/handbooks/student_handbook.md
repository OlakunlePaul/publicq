# 👨‍🎓 Student Handbook: Participating & Results

Welcome to your Student Dashboard! This is your gateway to the "Examination Hall" and your "Academic Record."

---

## 🏛️ Session 1: The Examination Hall (Taking Exams)
Participating in your scheduled assessments.

1.  **Login**: Use your unique **Admission Number** (e.g., `DISC/2026/0001`). 
2.  **Find Your Exam**: On your dashboard, any currently active or upcoming exams will appear in your "Hall."
3.  **Start Exam**: Click "Start" at the scheduled time. 
    *   *Important: Do not switch tabs or minimize the browser during the exam, as the system monitors your activity for the teacher.*
4.  **Submit**: Once finished, click **Submit**. Your marks are instantly sent to the **Result Office**.

---

## 📊 Session 2: My Academic Record (View Results)
Checking your performance.

1.  **Find Results**: Go to the **Report Cards** tab.
2.  **View Scores**: Once your teacher and manager have approved the marks, your scores will appear.
3.  **Detailed Report**: Open your **Individual Report Card** to see:
    *   Scores for CA (Continuous Assessment) and Exams.
    *   Letter Grades (A, B, C, etc.).
    *   Teacher and Principal comments.
4.  **Download**: Click **Download PDF** to save or print a official copy of your report card.
