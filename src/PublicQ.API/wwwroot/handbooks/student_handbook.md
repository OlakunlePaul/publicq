# 👨‍🎓 Student Handbook: Taking Exams & Results

Welcome to your **Student Dashboard** on ExamNova!

This portal is your personal, digital gateway to taking your school assessments and viewing your academic records. It is extremely easy to use. Follow these steps to get familiar with how it works.

<br/>

## 🏛️ Session 1: The Examination Hall
When it's time for a test or exam, this is where you'll go.

1. **Secure Login**  
   Always log in using the unique **Admission Number** given to you by the school (for example: `DISC/2026/0001`).
   
2. **Find Your Exam**  
   On your dashboard, check the "My Exams" area. Any exam that is currently active (or scheduled for today) will appear right there waiting for you.
   
3. **Start the Clock**  
   When the scheduled time arrives, click the **Start** button. The clock will begin counting down automatically.

> **⚠️ Important Rule for Exams:**  
> During a live exam, **do not switch tabs or minimize your browser window!** The system is equipped with monitoring tools, and your teacher will be notified if you leave the exam screen.

4. **Submit Your Work**  
   Once you have answered the questions, click **Submit**. Your answers are instantly transmitted to your teacher's grading office. 

<br/>

## 📊 Session 2: My Academic Record
You don't have to wait weeks for results or stand in long queues. Your performance records are delivered securely directly to you.

1. **Find Your Results**  
   Navigate to the **Report Cards** tab on your menu.
   
2. **View Your Grades**  
   As soon as your teacher and the school manager have officially approved the scores, they will magically appear here.
   
3. **Detailed Breakdown**  
   By opening your Report Card, you will see a detailed breakdown:
   * Your Continuous Assessment (CA) scores and Exam scores.
   * Your automated Letter Grades (like **A, B, C**).
   * Personal remarks and comments from your teachers and principal.

4. **Download or Print**  
   Need a physical copy? Use the **Download PDF** button to securely save or print an official, formatted copy of your report card.

> **🎉 You're All Set!**  
> Focus on your studies and let ExamNova handle the rest. Wishing you the best of luck in your assessments!
