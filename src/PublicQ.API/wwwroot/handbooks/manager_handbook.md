# 👨‍💼 Manager Handbook: School Oversight

Welcome to your **Manager Dashboard** on ExamNova! As a Manager, you are the chief architect of the platform. Your role is grouped into three simple phases: setting up the school structure, managing users, and overseeing results. 

Don't worry—everything is designed to be as straightforward as possible. Follow this guide to get started.

<br/>

## 🏛️ Phase 1: Building the School Structure
Before any exams can happen, the system needs to know how your school is organized. Think of this as laying the foundations.

1. **Create an Academic Session**  
   Go to **School Structure** > **Sessions** on your menu. Create the current session, such as *"2025/2026 Academic Session"*.
   
2. **Add Your Terms**  
   Under the session you just created, add your terms (e.g., *"First Term"*, *"Second Term"*).

3. **Set Up Classes & Subjects**  
   Define the classes in your school (e.g., *"Primary 1"*, *"JSS 3"*) and the subjects taught in each.

> **💡 Pro Tip:**  
> When creating classes, you can link them to a **Grading Schema**. This ensures the system automatically assigns accurate letter grades (like *A, B, C*) to students' scores!

<br/>

## 📋 Phase 2: User Registry & Admissions
Once the structure is set, it's time to bring your staff and students on board.

1. **Set the Admission Format**  
   Navigate to **Registration Format**. This determines how student IDs are generated. If you set it to `DISC/{YYYY}/{0000}`, a student might automatically get an ID like `DISC/2026/0001`.
   
2. **Register Your People**  
   Go to the **Users** tab. Click **Create New User**.
   * **Students:** Turn on the *Create as Student* switch. Leave the Admission Number blank, and the system will instantly generate one for them.
   * **Teachers:** Create their accounts and make sure to assign them the **Teacher** role.
   * **Parents:** Parents can also be signed up and securely linked to their children so they can view Report Cards later.

<br/>

## 📂 Phase 3: The Academic Vault
As exams conclude, you have the final authority to review performance and approve results.

1. **Access the Broadsheet**  
   Go to **Report Cards** and open the *Broadsheet View*. This is your master spreadsheet! You can view the continuous assessment and exam scores of every student in a class, all at a glance.

2. **Audit & Oversee**  
   Ensure that the teachers have properly input or synced all scores. 

3. **Review Report Cards**  
   Preview individual Report Cards. Once you are satisfied with the accuracy of the grading and teacher remarks, the results can be officially finalized.

> **🎉 You're Ready!**  
> Setting up a school has never been this easy. If you ever get stuck, just remember that the system is designed to gently guide you along. Happy Managing!
